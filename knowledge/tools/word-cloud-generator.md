## FAQs

- What are stop words?: Stop words are common words like 'the', 'is', 'and' that are filtered out because they don't convey meaningful content. This makes your word cloud more useful.
- How many words appear in the cloud?: The generator displays the top 50 most frequent words after filtering. This provides a good balance between detail and clarity.
- Can I use the word cloud in presentations?: Yes! Take a screenshot of the word cloud to use in presentations, reports, or social media. The visual representation works great for various purposes.
- Does it work with other languages?: Yes, the generator works with any language. However, stop word filtering is optimized for English, so you may see more common words in other languages.
