## FAQs

- What is aspect ratio?: Aspect ratio is the proportional relationship between width and height, expressed as width:height (e.g., 16:9 means 16 units wide for every 9 units tall).
- What ratio should I use for YouTube?: YouTube standard is 16:9. Use 1920x1080, 2560x1440, or 3840x2160 for best quality depending on your source footage.
- What about Instagram?: Instagram supports 1:1 (square), 4:5 (portrait), and 1.91:1 (landscape). Stories and Reels use 9:16 vertical format.
- How do I find an image's current ratio?: Enter the width and height of your image and the calculator will show you the simplified aspect ratio.
