# Text Case Converter - Uppercase, Lowercase & More

## Overview

Tired of retyping text just to change its capitalization? Transform any text between different capitalization styles with a single click.


## Key features

- UPPERCASE - Convert all letters to capitals
- lowercase - Convert all letters to small case
- Title Case - Capitalize first letter of each word
- Sentence case - Capitalize first letter of sentences
- One-click copy to clipboard
- Instant conversion with no delays

## How to use

1. Paste or type your text into the input area
2. Click the desired case conversion button
3. Your text is instantly converted
4. Copy the result to your clipboard
5. Clear and convert more text as needed

## FAQs

- What is Title Case?: Title Case capitalizes the first letter of every word in your text. It's commonly used for headlines, book titles, and headings in articles.
- Does this preserve my original text?: The converter displays the transformed text separately. Your original text remains in the input field until you clear it or type something new.
- Can this fix text copied from PDFs?: Yes! Text copied from PDFs often has incorrect capitalization. Simply paste it in and use the appropriate case conversion to fix it.
- Is there a character limit?: No, there's no character limit. You can convert large documents, though very long texts may take a moment to process.
