## FAQs

- What is the Twitter character limit?: Standard Twitter/X posts have a 280 character limit. Links count as 23 characters regardless of actual length. Premium users may have higher limits.
- Do emojis count as multiple characters?: Most emojis count as 2 characters due to Unicode encoding. Complex emojis (like family combinations) may count as more.
- How are threads calculated?: Threads are estimated by dividing your text into 280-character segments. Actual thread layout may vary based on word breaks.
- Does this account for links?: The counter shows raw character count. Remember that all URLs are counted as 23 characters by Twitter, regardless of actual length.
