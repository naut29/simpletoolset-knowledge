# Word Counter - Free Character & Word Count Tool

## Overview

Whether you're crafting the perfect tweet, writing a college essay, or optimizing your blog content for SEO, knowing your exact word count is essential. Our word counter instantly analyzes your text and provides a complete breakdown of words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, and estimated reading time.


## Key features

- Real-time word and character counting
- Sentence and paragraph detection
- Reading time estimation
- Works with any language or character set
- No file upload required - works instantly
- 100% private - text never leaves your browser

## How to use

1. Paste or type your text into the text area
2. Watch the statistics update in real-time as you type
3. View word count, character count, sentence count, and more
4. Use the clear button to reset and start fresh
5. Copy statistics to share or save for reference

- Is this word counter accurate?: Yes, our word counter uses precise algorithms to count words, characters, and sentences. It handles punctuation, special characters, and multiple spaces correctly.
