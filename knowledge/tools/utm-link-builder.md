## FAQs

- What are the required UTM parameters?: Source, medium, and campaign are required. Source identifies the referrer (e.g., facebook), medium identifies the marketing medium (e.g., cpc), and campaign identifies the specific campaign.
- What are term and content for?: Term is used for paid search keywords. Content differentiates similar content or links within the same ad, useful for A/B testing.
- Do UTM links work with any analytics?: UTM parameters are standardized and work with Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, and most marketing platforms that support campaign tracking.
- How should I name my parameters?: Use lowercase, consistent naming. Avoid spaces (use underscores or hyphens). Be specific but concise. Create a naming convention for your team.
